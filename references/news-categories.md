# News Categories

Quick classification guide. Read the input and match it to one or more categories.
Most inputs fit one primary category with secondary overlaps.

## Category Signals

### Macro/Policy
**Trigger words:** Fed, rates, CPI, inflation, GDP, employment, treasury, yields,
monetary policy, fiscal, stimulus, QE, QT, taper, FOMC, Powell, central bank,
sanctions, tariffs, trade war, G7, G20, IMF, debt ceiling
**Analytical lens:** Liquidity and risk appetite

### Regulatory
**Trigger words:** SEC, CFTC, DOJ, enforcement, lawsuit, settlement, legislation,
bill, act, compliance, license, ban, restrict, approve, ETF filing, Wells notice,
Gensler, Congress, Senate, hearing, subpoena, executive order
**Analytical lens:** Clarity vs uncertainty, winners vs losers

### Tech/Infrastructure
**Trigger words:** Upgrade, fork, EIP, v2/v3/v4, launch, mainnet, testnet, SDK,
API, protocol, Layer 2, rollup, zero knowledge, merge, sharding, throughput,
TPS, bridge, interoperability, smart contract, audit, open source
**Analytical lens:** Capability expansion, adoption potential

### Social/Cultural
**Trigger words:** Viral, trending, celebrity, Elon, influencer, community, DAO vote,
governance, scandal, controversy, hack (social), drama, ratio, engagement,
mainstream media, normie, adoption, retail
**Analytical lens:** Attention flows, narrative half-life

### On-Chain/Data
**Trigger words:** Whale, wallet, transfer, mint, burn, TVL, volume, liquidity,
outflow, inflow, exchange balance, staking, unstaking, smart money, Nansen,
Arkham, DeBank, gas, MEV, mempool
**Analytical lens:** Positioning, supply dynamics

### Earnings/Fundamentals
**Trigger words:** Revenue, earnings, users, DAU, MAU, growth, profit, loss,
burn rate, runway, token unlock, vesting, treasury, buyback, dividend,
market cap, FDV, P/E, valuation
**Analytical lens:** Fundamental value, growth trajectory

### Black Swan
**Trigger words:** Hack, exploit, rug, depeg, insolvency, bankrupt, collapse,
freeze, halt, emergency, zero-day, vulnerability, contagion, bank run,
withdrawal pause, FTX-style
**Analytical lens:** Contagion risk, systemic vs isolated

## Multi-Category Events

Some events span categories. Prioritize by market impact:

| Event Type | Primary | Secondary |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| SEC sues a protocol | Regulatory | Tech (if protocol-specific) |
| Fed announces rate decision | Macro | Earnings (affects growth) |
| Major hack of a DeFi protocol | Black Swan | Tech, On-Chain |
| Celebrity launches token | Social | On-Chain (if notable flows) |
| Protocol passes governance vote | Tech | Social (community signal) |
| Exchange gets licensed | Regulatory | Earnings (growth unlocked) |
| Whale moves to new protocol | On-Chain | Tech (protocol gaining traction) |
