## Macro Narratives (Cross-Asset)

### Risk-On / Liquidity Expansion
- Long: $BTC, $SOL, high-beta alts, $QQQ, growth equities
- Short: $TLT (bonds sell off), $DXY weakens, gold underperforms

### Risk-Off / Flight to Safety
- Long: $BTC (digital gold thesis), gold, $TLT, stablecoins, cash
- Short: Small caps, high-beta alts, memecoins, $IWM

### Dollar Weakness / De-dollarization
- Long: $BTC, gold, $XAU, commodity currencies
- Short: $DXY, dollar-denominated debt plays

### Rate Cuts / Dovish Fed
- Long: Growth, tech, crypto, $QQQ, duration ($TLT)
- Short: Banks (margins compress), $DXY

### Rate Hikes / Hawkish Fed
- Long: $DXY, banks, money markets
- Short: Growth, tech, crypto, high-duration

### Institutional Adoption
When TradFi moves into crypto — ETFs, custody, tokenization.
- Long: $BTC, $ETH (ETF assets), $COIN, $AAVE, RWA tokens
- Short: CEX tokens (DEX narrative strengthens), pure-play DeFi tokens that can't comply

### Institutional Trust Collapse
When scandals, corruption, or failures erode trust in institutions.
- Long: $BTC (exit from system), decentralized protocols, privacy tools
- Short: Bank stocks, centralized platforms, tokens associated with institutions

## Sector Rotation Cheat Sheet

When money flows INTO → it typically flows FROM:
- Memecoins → DeFi blue chips (risk appetite increasing)
- DeFi → Stablecoins (risk appetite decreasing)
- Alts → BTC (flight to quality within crypto)
- BTC → Alts (BTC dominance falling, "alt season")
- Crypto → Equities (crypto underperforming TradFi)
- Growth → Value (rate hike cycle)
- US → International (dollar weakening)
